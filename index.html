Game Heroes

PowersVR is a superheroic adventure available in Early Access for the HTC Vive and Oculus Rift. Step into the shoes of caped crusaders and supervillains as you explore a sandbox city filled with unique, unlockable superpowers and various challenges! Follow the game's open development as more and more content is added, leading to the release of the Story Campaign in 2018. If you'd like to fuel the game's development, get the game cheaper than on it's release day or just want to be a superhero in VR earlier you can pick your copy up on Steam or the Oculus store.
